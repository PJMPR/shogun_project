\subsection*{\textit{Wersja w języku angielskim (English version)}}

\textit{This course aims to provide students with comprehensive knowledge and skills necessary for designing and maintaining secure IT systems. By combining theoretical lectures with practical exercises, students are better prepared to face cybersecurity challenges in real-world environments. Students learn the basic concepts and principles of information security, such as confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. They learn how to design IT systems and infrastructure resistant to a variety of threats and attacks. The course also aims to develop an understanding of identity management and access control mechanisms, including the implementation of security policies. The course covers secure programming techniques and best practices that help prevent vulnerabilities from being introduced already at the software development stage. Students also learn how to respond to security incidents, including conducting incident analysis and reporting breaches.}
